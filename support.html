Your Impact

Where Your
Money Goes

Every dollar raised through your generosity and our community events stays right here in Berlin and the surrounding area. The Berlin Lions Club Charities (501c3) ensures complete transparency and accountability in how funds are used.

  • Berlin Upbeat & local youth programs
  • Scholarships for Berlin High School graduates
  • Veterans support & the Berlin Veterans Commission
  • Vision screenings for local school children
  • American School for the Deaf & Lions Low Vision Center
  • Local food banks & disaster relief efforts
  • Berlin Relay for Life & Hospital for Special Care
