Berlin Lions 2nd Annual Christmas Tree Walk

The Berlin Lions are excited to host the 2nd Annual Christmas Tree Walk at the Berlin Lions Fairgrounds on December 6th, 7th, 13th, and 14th from 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM. Each evening, we'll spotlight a different local charity.
‍Enjoy complimentary snacks and hot chocolate provided by the Lions Club as you stroll through the festive display. Come share the holiday spirit while giving back to the community!

Berlin Lions Donate $12,000 to Support Local Veterans

The Berlin Lions presented the Berlin Veterans Commission with a $12,000 check from the proceeds of their 5th Annual Luminary display held on November 9th at Veterans Park and throughout the town.The Berlin Lions wish to thank all the individuals and businesses that supported the event. This was truly a collaborative effort within our community that made this event an overwhelming success and will provide much needed support to our local Veterans during the Holiday season and the coming year.
