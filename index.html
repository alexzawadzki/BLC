Holiday Event · 2024

Berlin Lions 2nd Annual Christmas Tree Walk

The Berlin Lions are excited to host the 2nd Annual Christmas Tree Walk at the Berlin Lions Fairgrounds on December 6th, 7th, 13th, and 14th from 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM. Each evening, we'll spotlight a different local charity. Enjoy complimentary snacks and hot chocolate as you stroll through the festive display. Come share the holiday spirit while giving back to the community!

Community Impact · 2024

Berlin Lions Donate $12,000 to Support Local Veterans

The Berlin Lions presented the Berlin Veterans Commission with a $12,000 check from the proceeds of their 5th Annual Luminary display held on November 9th at Veterans Park. The Berlin Lions wish to thank all the individuals and businesses that supported this truly collaborative community effort — providing much-needed support to our Veterans during the holiday season and the coming year.
